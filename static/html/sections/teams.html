incubates
is for people who want to ideate, develop, and execute design projects within a challenging yet supportive creative team. This team involves flexible projects - the subject matter, timeline, and team itself are up to you. You'll be supported by our design director Sheri to flesh out ideas, stay on track, and get any other help you'll need.

Check out incubates' work here.
experience
is for people who want to shape the design experience of the Cornell community. You'll design events that make people think, whether that be bringing in speakers, organizing workshops, or building popups. Experience can be physical, digital, or conceptual. you'll be working with Miriam.
curation
is for people who want to show the world what we're up to. there is both an editorial component and a web component, so apply if you are interested in either. On the editorial side, you'll create a couple of editorial pieces. On the web side, you'll help maintain our website and build a new platform to show off our members' work (including yours). You'll be working with David.

Check out our editorial here.
graphic communication
attempts to answer the need for a graphic design course at Cornell. Members will work on prompt-led projects and meet to discuss/participate in critiques. Commission work is also occasionally available. GC is an experiment, an exploration, (and a place to build portfolios, fingers crossed). Try to have fun, though. You'll be working with Marc.
